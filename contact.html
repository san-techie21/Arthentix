Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if an artwork is authentic?

Every artwork on Arthentix comes with a blockchain-verified Certificate of Authenticity. You can verify any certificate using our verification tool.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy on all purchases. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll arrange a free pickup and full refund.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How does art rental work?

Choose any artwork from our rental collection, select your rental period, and we'll deliver it to your door. Full insurance included.

Can I commission a custom artwork?

Absolutely! Many of our artists accept commissions. Contact us with your requirements and we'll connect you with suitable artists.

How do I become a seller on Arthentix?

Visit our Seller Portal to apply. We review all applications to ensure quality and authenticity standards are met.
