Priya Sharma is a contemporary Indian artist whose work explores the intersection of traditional Indian aesthetics and modern abstract expressionism. Born and raised in Mumbai, her art reflects the vibrant chaos and serene beauty of urban Indian life.

After graduating from Sir J.J. School of Art, Priya spent several years traveling across India, studying various folk art forms including Madhubani, Warli, and Pattachitra. These influences are evident in her work, which combines bold colors with intricate patterns and deep emotional resonance.
