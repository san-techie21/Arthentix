Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What if the artwork gets damaged?

Every rental is fully insured. In case of accidental damage, our insurance covers repairs or replacement at no additional cost to you.

Can I buy the artwork I'm renting?

Yes! With our Rent-to-Own program, up to 50% of your rental payments can be applied toward the purchase price.

How does delivery work?

We provide white-glove delivery across major Indian cities. Our team handles packing, shipping, and professional installation.

What's the minimum rental period?

The minimum rental period is 3 months. After that, you can swap artwork, extend your rental, or purchase the piece.

Is the artwork authenticated?

Every artwork comes with a blockchain-verified certificate of authenticity from Arthentix, ensuring you're renting genuine original art.

Can I swap artwork during my rental?

Yes, longer rental terms include free artwork swaps. You can also swap anytime by paying a small rotation fee.
